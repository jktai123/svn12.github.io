Behringer X32 Digital Owners Manual

·         Article #1723876

 

·         Updated on May 25, 2019 at 2:52 PM

The Behringer X32 is a great mixer for all purposes, whether it be for your home recording studio or mixing live performances. To answer all of your X32 questions, take a look at the X32 manual.

Also, check out this this wiki page for the Behringer X32 written by Behringer Tech Support that is designed to be easy to avigate and informative. In it you’ll find extensive information about the X32. If you need to know how to do something on your X32, chances are you’ll find it there.

Lastly, Behringer’s X32 Resources Page

 is filled with great videos to provide additional help including routing tutorials and feature explanations. Lastly, you can access their downloads page for all driver and firmware downloads for the X32

If you have any more questions about the X32, check out the helpful links below.
